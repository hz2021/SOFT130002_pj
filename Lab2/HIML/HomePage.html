Mona Lisa Leonardo da Vinci



The Mona Lisa (or La Gioconda) is a half-length portrait of a woman by the Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci, which has been acclaimed as \"the best known, the most visited, the most written about, the most sung about, the most parodied work of art in the world.\"

The Milkmaid Jan Vermeer



The Milkmaid is an oil-on-canvas painting of a \"milkmaid\", in fact a domestic kitchen maid, by the Dutch artist Johannes Vermeer. It is now in the Rijksmuseum in Amsterdam, Netherlands, which regards it as \"unquestionably one of the museum\'s finest attractions\"

Starry Night Vincent Van Gogh



In the later part of his life, Van Gogh committed himself to an asylum in Saint Remy de Provence. The Starry Night was the view from the window in Van Gogh’s sanitarium bedroom.
